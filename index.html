The Space

More Than Play.
This Is Where Kids Actually Grow.

Little Bee is an indoor play space for kids ages 0–6 in Lexington, SC. We keep sessions small on purpose — so your child gets to actually play, not wait in line or compete for space.

🧠

Built Around the Senses

Touch, sound, movement, light — every corner of this space has something to discover. We built it around how kids actually experience the world.

🛡️

Safe by Design

We cap every session so it never gets chaotic. Grip socks required, waivers on file, staff always on the floor.

🌈

Inclusive & Welcoming

Kids with sensory sensitivities belong here. We keep sessions calm and low-key on purpose — so every child can show up and just play.

☕

The Buzz Café

Grab a snack or a drink at The Buzz while you're here. Kid-friendly bites and drinks available every session.

Good to Know

Before You Visit

📋

Waiver Required

Everyone needs a signed waiver before entering the play area. Fill it out online before you come so you're not doing paperwork at the door.

🧦

Socks Required

Grip socks are required for all children. Forget yours? We sell them at the front for $3.

📅

Walk-ins Welcome

Walk-ins are welcome, but a spot isn't guaranteed based on current capacity. Book ahead to lock in your time.

⏰

Arrive on Time

Your session ends at the same time regardless of when you walk in. Give yourself a few extra minutes when you arrive.

🍽️

No Outside Food

No outside food or drinks during open play. Outside food is allowed at private parties in designated areas only.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parents Stay On Site

One adult per child is included. Extra adults are $3 each. This is a parent-supervised space — not a drop-off facility.

Policies

Cancellation & Refund Policy

We keep things fair for everyone. Please read before booking.

🎠 Play Sessions

  • All session bookings are non-refundable.
  • Cancellations made at least 12 hours in advance may be rescheduled one time as a courtesy.
  • Cancellations with less than 12 hours notice will be forfeited.
  • No-shows are non-refundable and cannot be rescheduled.

⚡ Busy Bee Express

  • All express sessions are non-refundable.
  • One reschedule allowed with 12+ hours notice.
  • No-shows are forfeited.

🎉 Private Parties

  • Minimum 7 day advance booking required.
  • A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to reserve your party date.
  • Remaining balance is due before the party starts.
  • Final guest count is due 48 hours before the event.
  • Cancellations made less than 7 days before the event will result in loss of deposit.

💛 Memberships

  • Memberships are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  • You can cancel at any time — your membership stays active through the end of the current billing cycle.
  • No refunds or partial credits for unused visits.

⚠️ Refund Exceptions

Refunds are only issued in the following cases:

  • Facility closure due to emergency or unforeseen circumstances
  • A session canceled by our staff

In these cases, guests will be offered a full refund or the option to reschedule.

All policies apply regardless of reason, including illness, schedule conflicts, or weather. By booking a session, party, or membership, you agree to this policy.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you book a session, purchase a membership, inquire about a party, or contact us, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Child's name, age, and any relevant health or sensory information you choose to share
  • Payment information (processed securely through our booking platform)
  • Signed liability waiver information
  • Communications you send us through our contact form or email

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process bookings, memberships, and party reservations
  • Communicate with you about your visits, sessions, and inquiries
  • Maintain waiver records as required for liability purposes
  • Send occasional updates about events, promotions, or policy changes
  • Improve our services and the experience at Little Bee

3. Photos & Videos

We may take photos or videos inside the facility for marketing and social media purposes. You may opt out of this at any time by noting it on your waiver at check-in or by contacting us directly. We will never publish identifiable photos of your child without your consent.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers (such as our booking platform and payment processor) solely to operate our business. These providers are required to keep your information confidential.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. Payment processing is handled by secure third-party platforms. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

We collect limited information about children solely for the purpose of providing our services safely. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. All information related to minors is provided by their parent or legal guardian.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time. To make a request, contact us at info@busybeesplayhousesc.com or call (803) 520-0016.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

  • Phone: (803) 520-0016
  • Address: 5483-B Sunset Blvd, Lexington, SC 29072
